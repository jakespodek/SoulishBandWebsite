Soulish is a 6-piece Indie Soul band hailing from Toronto. Soulish blends the rhythmic and storytelling aspects of Hip-Hop with the raw energy and experi- mental edge of Indie Rock. With a diverse cast of members, an eclectic and wide range of influences permeate our collective sound, resulting in music that sounds both organic and sample-based at the same time. The variety of genres and sounds we incorporate is representative of the cumulative experiences of all the mem- bers of the band, and of the cultural melt- ing pot that is the City of Toronto.

Lead by Cole Solish as the Rhythm Guitar- ist and primary lyricist, Soulish continues to explore genre-bending sound. Lead Vocal- ist Chris Hylton brings his classical vocal training to provide a lens into the stories of a band growing both as people and profes- sional musicians in Toronto. The Rhythm section is comprised of brothers Hector and Ernesto Alonso on Drums and Bass respectively. Their musical connection is unmatched and creates the backbone of our sonic chemistry. The band is rounded out by life-long friends Jakob Goldstein and Joshua Napal carrying the Lead Guitar and Synthesizer responsibilities. Their unique approach to song craft and tone-building are key in establishing our musical presence.

Soulish is currently working with Toronto based musician, professor (York University), and producer, Robb Cappelletto, on a series of new songs to be released at the end of 2022 and into early 2023.
